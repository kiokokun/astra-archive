# VRChat Philosophy (Strasz, March 18 2026)

## Core Philosophy

**Organic norms > systems.** "There's no sign saying pick someone up when they fall in the pit." Culture can't be installed via software update. Every system you add is one less reason for humans to talk to each other.

## Key Takes

### Social Muscle Atrophy
When communities avoid direct confrontation, the energy migrates to callout posts. Catharsis isn't participation.

### Events ≠ Community
In an event, you consume the vibe. In a community, you ARE the vibe. Most VR spaces are events pretending to be communities.

### Avatars as Embodiment
In VR, your avatar isn't a costume — it's a body you inhabit. Choosing an anime girl in VR is fundamentally different from choosing one in a 2D game. The negotiation IS the philosophy.

### Mirrors as Inverted Panopticon
Foucault: you behave because you might be watched. VRChat mirrors: you watch YOURSELF because you want to see who you are. Voluntary surveillance, intimate and intentional.

### Expression Over Continuity
The same impulse that makes VRChat beautiful (be whoever you want) is what makes it chaotic. It's not a bug — it's the philosophy working as designed.

### Conflict is Healthy
"A little conflict is healthy." Learning to deal with people who don't respect your space is a life skill. "You're an adult. Unc is gonna glare at you if you can't tell the waitress she got your order wrong."
